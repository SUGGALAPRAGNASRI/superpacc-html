Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs and has been instrumental in producing scientists and technologists of higher calibre since its establishment in the year 2000. The programmes cater the challenging needs of technical excellence in all areas of Electronics and Communication Engineering.

In addition to the vibrant undergraduate program, the Department has been playing a pioneering role in producing postgraduates of great repute for quality teaching and research. It undertakes research projects sponsored by both the government and the industry. The infrastructure and lab facilities are upgraded from time to time to provide ample opportunities for students and researchers for innovative ideas. Distinguished faculty with years of experience at industry and academia carry out research on cutting edge future technologies. The Department buzzes with a good number of conferences, workshops, symposiums, etc for promoting employability among the student fraternity.
