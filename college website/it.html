The Department of Information Technology was started in the year 1998. The intake of the department is 120. The department got accreditation by National Board of Accreditation in 2007 and reaccreditation in 2012. Once again it is provisionally accredited for 3 years under OBE in the year 2016 and got 4th time accreditation under OBE Tier 1 in 2019 for 3 academic years. The Department has 24 eminent faculty among them 11 are having with Ph.D - Doctorate degrees, 13 are having M.Tech degrees in CSE, IT, IST and Software Engineering. The Department has 3 Professors, 1 Associate Professor 1 Senior Assistant Professor and 19 Assistant Professors. The department is conducting Infosys-campus connect programme /Spring Board to train the students so as to make them industry ready. NPTEL online certification course and IIT Bombay Spoken tutorials are initiated in the institute and many from IT department were certified in various courses.

AICTE has sanctioned an amount of Rs. 7.25 Lakhs to establish Entrepreneurship Development Cell coordinated by Dr.J.Rajendra Prasad, former Head of IT. DST New Delhi Sanctioned an amount of Rs. 6 Lakhs to Dr.B.V.Subba Rao, Professor and Head of IT for Big Data Initiative under BDI-Division of DST. Dr D Kavitha, Dr. Y Suresh, Dr. K.Pavan kumar got minor research projects from UGC. Faculty of IT have published 17 patents and continuing the Patents filing. All the faculty members are enthusiastically involved in research activities. The faculty published more than 150 research articles (out of which 30 Articles are in Scopus/SCI) in various conferences, national and international journals.

The Department is periodically organizing seminars, conferences, faculty development programs, workshops and guest lecturers for the benefit of both the students and the staff. All Passed out students from the department got more than 75% of placements. 2016 passed out students got 66 placement offers, 2017 passed outstudents got 99 placement offers, 2018 passed out students got 116 placement offers and 2019 passed outs got 112 placement offers, 2020 passed outs got 115 placements offers and 2021 passes out got 191 offers with 7.60 lakhs as highest pay package and 4.01 is the average pay package. These batches of students got more than 90 % pass in their academics. Students are also actively participating in national Level competitions like SIH, AICTE-Chhatra Vishwakarma awards, and various events conducted by NIRF rank below 200 institutions, students are actively participating in various professional society activities under CSI, ISTE chapters.
