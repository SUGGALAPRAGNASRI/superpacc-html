Education is the process by which individual is shaped to fit into the society and which maintain at advances the social order. Education in its broad sense means preparation for life. Physical education and Education are two sides of the same coin. Physical education is an integral part of total education. Process which has its aim - "the development of physically, mentally, emotionally and socially fit citizens through the medium of physical activities which have been related with a view of realizing these out comes".

Physical education is an organized experiences related to general education when attempts to lead the individual and the group in to situations after doing opportunities for the learning of neuromuscular skills, requisition of organic vigor's - strength ,endurance ,agility, flexibility, power at balance-development of desirable social attitudes and the enjoyment of creative expression through big muscle activities.
